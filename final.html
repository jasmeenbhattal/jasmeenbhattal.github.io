How to use Five Nights At Freddy's Program!
Five Nights at Freddy's is a game that involves the player taking on the role of a security guard
who must survive five nights while monitoring a pizzeria filled with animatronic characters that
move around at night. The objective of the game is to survive until 6:00 AM while avoiding being
caught by the animatronics.
To play the game, the player must use security cameras to monitor the various rooms in the pizzeria
and track the movements of the animatronics. The player can move through the various rooms checking
to see if the animatronics move. If the animatronic reaches the office the game will be over, try to survive
until 6:00 AM by clicking on the animatronics when you see them in the rooms. With each night, the game
becomes increasingly difficult, as the animatronics become faster.
